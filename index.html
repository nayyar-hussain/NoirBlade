About Us

London's Finest
Hair Atelier

Noir & Blade was born from a singular obsession: to bring true Parisian artistry to the streets of London. Our master stylists have trained across Europe, combining heritage techniques with modern innovation.

We believe every visit should feel like a ritual — from the warm greeting to the final reveal. Located in Mayfair, our salon is a sanctuary of calm in a busy city.
